Meet VoltMat

VoltMat is a foldable charging mat with a built-in touchscreen. It keeps your devices powered wherever life takes you. No messy cords, no outlet battles, and no dead batteries during class, study sessions, or sports practice.

USB-C Port

For devices that still need a cable, VoltMat has you covered.

Internal Battery

Charge VoltMat ahead of time and take it anywhere, or plug it in when outlets are available.

Touchscreen Control

Monitor battery levels and prioritize power distribution with a simple tap.

Smart Touchscreen

The built-in touchscreen is more than a simple display. It lets you control how much power goes to each device, shows the battery status of VoltMat itself, and provides extras like weather updates, time display, and simple games to pass the time between classes.

Durability You Can Trust

VoltMat is built with a tough, flexible rubber exterior that resists wear and tear. The reinforced touchscreen is designed to withstand daily use without cracking. Fold it into a small rectangle and toss it in your backpack. It is built to handle the bumps of student life.

Extras That Make Life Easier

Beyond charging, VoltMat adds small conveniences. The touchscreen can show the time, weather, and even simple games. These extras make VoltMat feel personal and fun, not just another tech accessory.

Why VoltMat is Better

Traditional charging mats are limited. They break when bent, charge only one or two devices, and require constant access to a wall outlet. VoltMat solves all of this with flexibility, multiple charging options, a built-in battery, and touchscreen control. It is not just a charger. It is a portable charging station.
